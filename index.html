Unveil the Origins of Halloween

Step back in time to explore Halloween’s haunting roots, from ancient rituals to modern-day traditions.

Where it All Began: The Celtic Festival of Samhain

Halloween’s story began with the ancient Celts, who celebrated Samhain to mark the end of harvest and welcome winter. Bonfires lit up the night as people donned disguises to avoid the gaze of wandering spirits.

The Medieval Transformation

In the Middle Ages, Halloween was shaped by Christian observances like All Saints’ Day and All Souls’ Day. Communities honored the dead, giving rise to customs such as soul cakes and early trick-or-treating.
